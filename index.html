Home

While UCLA's students have mostly had a positive experience, they've all shared one dire problem, parking. UCLA parking has been a burden for many students due to numerous reasons. Parking is extremely difficult for students to find, as it's usually very busy. Unfortunately, the bus won't provide any help, as there is no one bus to UCLA, there're only two, vastly overcomplicating the process for students. Despire all these difficulties, us here at UCLA present a novel solution, ride sharing. Students can carpool with people based on needs. Some of these needs are scheduling, class times, and extracurriculars.

Rewards

The rewards for carpooling are that you will get 50% off when you park on the campus. Also, you can be 15 minutes late to your first class and you won't be called tardy.
